# Pump Pressure Diagnostics

## Cavitation

Pressure oscillation combined with inlet rattling, low reservoir level, or restricted inlet flow supports pump cavitation.

## Seal leak

A pressure decrease combined with visible fluid around a pump shaft seal supports `seal_leak`.

## Mechanical failure

Low pressure accompanied by grinding, overheating, abnormal vibration, or failure to rotate may support `mechanical_jam` or another mechanical failure.

A pressure decrease alone does not establish a physical leak.
